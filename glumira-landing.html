Exponential decay model

Every insulin type has a defined duration of action. IOB Hunter calculates residual insulin to the minute, for every injection in your history.

IOB(t) = dose × e^(−λt)
λ = ln(2) / (DOA × 0.5)
📐

ISF / ICR framework

Insulin Sensitivity Factor and Insulin-to-Carb Ratio derived from Total Daily Dose, with time-of-day modifiers for overnight and dawn sensitivity.

ISF = 100 / TDD (100 Rule)
ICR = 500 / TDD (500 Rule)
🎯

Stacking risk scoring

A 0–100 risk score from all active IOB across multiple doses. Four clinical risk zones guide safe decision-making with colour-coded alerts.

score = min(100, (totalIOB / basalDose) × 100)
