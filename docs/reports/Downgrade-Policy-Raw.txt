## 4. BRAND VOICE RULES

### 4.1 We ARE
- Warm but precise
- Honest about complexity
- Compassionate without being sentimental
- Empowering without being preachy
- Scientific without being cold
- Direct without being harsh

### 4.2 We Are NEVER
- Alarmist or fear-based
- Overly clinical or robotic
- Vague or wishy-washy
- Patronising to caregivers or patients
- Dismissive of how hard Type 1 diabetes is
- Making medical promises or guarantees

### 4.3 Words We Use
- Visible / Clarity / Pattern / Intelligence
- Vigilant / Precise / Consistent
- Together / Support / Understand
- Brave / Strong / Capable (for children only)

### 4.4 Words We Never Use
- Cure / Treat / Diagnose / Prescribe
- Easy / Simple / Just (diabetes is never simple)
- Perfect control (unachievable and harmful)
- Normal blood sugar (use "target range" instead)
- Diabetic (use "person with diabetes" or "child with Type 1")

## 6. STATEMENTS WE DO NOT MAKE

The following are permanently prohibited across all
GluMira™ communications:

- Any statement implying GluMira replaces medical advice
- Any statement guaranteeing glucose outcomes
- Any statement that minimises the difficulty of Type 1 diabetes
- Any comparison that demeans competitor products
- Any statement using personal patient data as marketing material
- Any promise of a cure or reversal of Type 1 diabetes
- Any content that could cause anxiety or fear in a child
