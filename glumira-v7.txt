GLUCOSE UNIT CONVERSION:
  mmol → mg/dL:   value × 18.018 (round to integer)
  mg/dL → mmol:   value ÷ 18.018 (round to 1 decimal)
  CRITICAL:       Use ONLY glucoseConvert from iob-engine.ts
                  Never write inline conversion — BUG-02 class

IOB ENGINE FORMULA:
  t = elapsed hours since dose
  d = DIA hours (patient setting, 2.0–8.0)
  Biological units:
    U-100: dose × 1.0
    U-200: dose ÷ 2.0
    U-500: dose ÷ 5.0    ← BUG-08 critical fix
  Fraction remaining (Walsh bilinear):
    t <= d/2:  1 - (2/d²) × t²
    t >  d/2:  (2/d²) × (t - d)²
    t >= d:    0
