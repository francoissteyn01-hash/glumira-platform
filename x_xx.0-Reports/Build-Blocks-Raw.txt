## 1. PHILOSOPHY

Downgrading must never feel like punishment.
It must never cause anxiety about data loss.
It must never surprise the user.

The user must always know exactly what they will lose
before they confirm a downgrade — clearly, honestly,
and without pressure tactics.

GluMira™ Free is a genuine product.
It is not a crippled version designed to frustrate.
It covers safety. It covers logging. It covers the basics
every person with Type 1 diabetes needs.

## 4. DATA RETENTION POLICY

### 4.1 The Rule
**No data is ever deleted on downgrade.**

All historical data — glucose logs, bolus logs, meal logs,
patterns, reports — is retained in full.

The user simply loses the ability to generate new
advanced outputs until they upgrade again.

### 4.2 What This Means in Practice
- A user who downgrades from AI to Free can still
  see all their historical AI reports
- They cannot generate new AI reports until they upgrade
- Their IOB Hunter™ history is preserved
- Their Mira education progress and badges are preserved
- Their streak is preserved

### 4.3 What We Tell the User
> "Your data is safe. Every log, every report, every badge
> stays with you. You simply won't be able to generate
> new advanced reports until you upgrade again.
> GluMira™ never deletes your history."

---

## 5. DOWNGRADE COMMUNICATION — UI RULES

### 5.1 Before Downgrade — Warning Screen
The user must see a clear list of what they will lose
BEFORE they confirm. This screen must:

- List every feature being lost in plain language
- Show the DATA RETENTION promise prominently
- Offer a 30-day pause option before full downgrade
- Never use guilt language or dark patterns
- Never show countdown timers or pressure tactics
- Include a single clear CTA: "I understand, downgrade my account"

### 5.2 Approved Warning Screen Text
> "Before you downgrade, here is what will change."
> [Feature list]
> "Your data stays with you — always."
> "Want more time? Pause your subscription for 30 days instead."

### 5.3 After Downgrade — Confirmation Screen
> "You are now on GluMira Free."
> "Your history is safe and your streak is intact."
> "Upgrade any time to unlock your full toolkit again."

### 5.4 Prohibited UI Patterns
- No red warning colours on the downgrade screen
- No language like "Are you sure?" repeated more than once
- No automatic emails after downgrade reminding them
  what they lost — one email only, one time
- No pop-ups after downgrade pushing an upgrade

---

## 6. UPGRADE PROMPTS — IN-APP RULES

When a Free or Pro user encounters a locked feature
the prompt must be:

> "This feature is available on GluMira [Pro/AI]."
> "See what's included →"

### Prohibited Upgrade Prompt Language
- Never: "Unlock this feature NOW"
- Never: "You are missing out"
- Never: "Upgrade to stop losing data"
- Never: Fear-based or urgency-based language
- Never: More than one upgrade prompt per session

---

## 7. CLINICAL LICENSE DOWNGRADE

Clinical license downgrades follow the same data
retention rules. Patient data is never deleted.
The clinician retains read-only access to all
historical patient records for 12 months after downgrade
for medico-legal compliance purposes.
